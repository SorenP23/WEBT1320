Take A Read, They Don't Bite!

Here are some cool facts about crocodiles. Crocodiles sometimes sleep with their mouths wide open They have the most powerful bite force in the animal kingdom They swallow stones to help digestion. Baby Crocodiles make noise inside their eggs before they hatch. Crocodiles have a V-Shaped snout.

Here are also some cool facts about Alligators. Temperature determines the gender in an Alligator. Alligators are very vocal. They can climb trees. A group of Alligators is called a congregation. Alligators have a U-Shaped snout.
