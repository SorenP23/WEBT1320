The Trucking Industry

Welcome! Enjoy This Information With Your Eyes.

The trucking industry is a very important industry. It is one of the most important industries. Currently there is a shortage due to the low pay and terrible companies. It is very hard to find good companies to work for nowadays. Some truckers enjoy what they do and have been doing it for upwards of 40 years. It is a very difficult and demanding job in terms of time and commitment, especially if they own their own rig. Most semi trucks cost upwards of 250,000 dollars, the driver have to calculate what their mantinence cost is per mile and other stuff like insurance and fuel and charge a set price per mile, or find load that pay that or greater.

Some parts of the trucking life style are a big exciting. Some examples might be exploring new places and seeing beautiful scenery. Some truckers are learn photography and videography to capture their cool moments in trucking. Some document their journeys on YouTube! It may seem like a cool career, but it is definitly not for everyone. It is important to appreciate the ones who do this for a living.
