We Got A Few

A truck simulator is nothing without the places to drive. so the game prides itself in the many states available. While we dont have all fifty states, we have quite a few to start off, with more on the way.

The current states are: Idaho, Montana, Oregon, Utah, Arizona, Nevada, Texas, California, Wyoming, Washington, New Mexico, Oklahoma. Kansas is on the way!
