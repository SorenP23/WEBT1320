We Got Some Weird Ones!

Some Crocodiles and Alligators are quite weird and they adapt to their environments. Some of the weirdest Crocodiles are the Gharial is one one the largest fish nomming crocodiles with a very slender snout. The Slender Snouted Crocodile is another with a very weird head shape with the slender snout. It is cool to see how they adapt to the different food they eat and the environments.

There are also a few strange Alligators as well. One example is the Chinese Alligator which has a stubby snoot. Another is the Albino American Alligator which is completely white, while not a special species, it's just Albino There are not many Alligators, but these are some of the strange Alligators.
